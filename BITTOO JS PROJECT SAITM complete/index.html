Welcome SAITM

St. Andrews Institute of Technology & Management is one of the prestigious colleges in India based in the industrial hub of India - Gurgaon, Delhi (NCR). SAITM offers a number of UG and PG courses in the field of technology & management. Spread across 22 acres of land, SAITM provides an environment-friendly campus with a globally acknowledged infrastructure that accommodates students from all over India and foreign countries. The classrooms at St. Andrews Institute are centrally air-conditioned and equipped with modern teaching tools to complement the overall development of students. We at SAITM believe that instilling moral values and ethics is an essential part of imparting quality education. Therefore, we equally focus on building students’ character as much as their skills. We provide a growth-oriented environment for students to facilitate their personal as well as their professional development. SAITM is affiliated with MDU and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).

Course

BCA (3 years )

St. Andrew’s Institute of Technology & Management (SAITM) is a renowned BCA college in Delhi that offers 3 years undergraduate BCA course to students who want to pursue their careers in computer-related fields and meet the BCA course eligibility criteria set by SAITM. Subjects included in the BCA syllabus mainly cover various programming languages, operating systems, their applications, and computer networking. Students who meet the BCA course eligibility and get enrolled at SAITM for pursuing BCA are given wide exposure to the IT industry. Also, the BCA (Bachelor of Computer Application) course at SAITM equally focuses on imparting practical knowledge and enhancing students’ soft skills.

BBA (3 years )

LaBachelor of Business Administration or BBA at SAITM is one of the trending bachelor’s degree programs that students opt for after completing school. The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) course offered by SAITM is comprehensive and inclusive of various aspects of new-age learning. Saitm is one of the top BBA colleges in Delhi that provides quality education along with opportunities for students to grow in all domains of life. BBA students at SAITM get complete exposure to the latest business practices and industry trends. The BBA course duration is 3 years which includes six semesters in which students are taught various BBA course subjects.

MBA

MBA is a 2-Year regular program available for graduates interested in pursuing a career in Business and Administration. Approved and accredited by AICTE and MDU respectively, SAITM's MBA is a coveted course with limited intake of 60 students. The purpose of MBA Degree Program is to train the students in different functional areas of the management and ensure the holistic development of all individuals and the organization.

Global MBA

SAITM is a catalyst in creating a learning process with a focus on polishing the entrepreneurial, managerial, and leadership qualities of budding talent with a focus on shaping society. Global Master of Business Administration (MBA) is a 2-Year Full-Time AICTE approved and MDU affiliated program. The program is designed as per the specific needs of the corporate and focuses on imparting the skills like Communication, Analytical, Negotiation, Counselling, Business Development, and Entrepreneurial Skills that help the students to learn how to address real-time organizational challenges effectively.
